Club rules require members to provide their own towels for use at the pools and beach.

Our pool rules state that no floats of any kind are permitted in the pools. The lone exception is 'water wings' and similar devices for young children in order to maintain balance.

Personal tubes are not permitted for use in the lazy river. Please use only one tube per person when visiting the lazy river to assure that there are enough tubes available for all guests who wish to use the amenity.

Personal size coolers only may be brought into the pool deck. Food and beverages are available for purchase at the Tiki Bar at the Main Pool. Larger coolers should be stored in your villa or vehicle — please do not leave them outside the pool gates where other people can help themselves.

Coolers found outside the gates may have a courtesy notice inserted explaining the need to return the cooler to a villa or vehicle. Coolers unmoved after 1 hour will be taken to the front desk for storage.

Local health code for commercial pools prohibits food or drink in the pool or on the wet deck. No items may be left near the edge of the water, or in any walkway where it might become a tripping hazard.

Note: It is hazardous to consume alcohol before or while using a hot tub.

Both of our pools are smoke-free. Smokers will need to exit the pool deck to smoke.

The Signature Pool is 'The Quiet Pool,' and is available to all guests. Located at Building Three, this pool is intended as a quiet "oasis" pool. All guests and all ages are welcome — we ask only that everyone observe a few basic guidelines:

  • Anyone under the age of 12 years must be accompanied by an adult (family member, guardian, or babysitter).
  • Personal conduct must be such that the safety of self and others is preserved.
  • As this is the quiet pool, there is no throwing balls or other items.
  • Rough play and intentional splashing is not permitted.
  • Electronic audio devices are permitted through ear buds/earphones & kept at a low volume.

Please don't bring any speakers to the pools. We have a PA system that plays music at the pool decks all day. If you bring a speaker to the pool, you will be asked to remove it.
